  • 📖 Overview

    🔹 This project provides a powerful workflow for creating personalized AI chatbots based on real human conversation styles. By exporting WhatsApp chat data, the tool cleans and processes the message history into a fine-tuning format for Large Language Models (LLMs). Users can then train or fine-tune models to mimic specific personalities, response styles, or collective group knowledge.
     

    💻 Tech stack & More

    ✅ NLP (Natural Language Processing)

    ✅ Conversational AI
