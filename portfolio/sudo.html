Sudo Messenger

🌐 The Vision

Sudo is a revolutionary Web3-based messaging platform that empowers users to connect, communicate, and collaborate with privacy, decentralization, and ownership in mind.

Login seamlessly using your crypto wallet—no phone number, no email, no compromise. Your wallet is your identity.

🔒 Privacy & Security

End-to-End Encryption

All messages are fully encrypted using the Signal Protocol for maximum privacy.

Wallet Identity

No personal data required. Use your crypto wallet as your unique, decentralized identity.

🛠️ Technical Architecture

Flutter / BLoC LiveKit (WebRTC Calling) Centrifugo (Real-time Messaging) Kafka (Event Streaming) Isar (Msg Synchronization) Signal Protocol Web3 Wallet Auth
